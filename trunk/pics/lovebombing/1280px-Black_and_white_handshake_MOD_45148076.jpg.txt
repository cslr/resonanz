Description	
English: A member of the International Military Assistance Training Team Sierra Leone (IMATT(SL)) and the Republic of Sierra Leone Armed Forces (RSLAF) are shown shaking hands.
The IMATT(SL) is based in Freetown, Sierra Leone where their role is to observe the RSLAF and provide them with support, training and assistance.
